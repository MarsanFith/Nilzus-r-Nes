What's your first thought when you think of New Zealand? Some think of the beautiful landscapes, most others think of the copious amounts of sheep, and a few can't even find it on the map.

But if you are able to find us, then you've most likely heard of New Zealand's clean green appearance, our country's tourism slogan being, "100% pure". This makes it seem that New Zealand is a healthy place for people to live, which is...sort of true.

It's true that we are 100% nuclear-free, and our air quality is pretty good, but what everyone, including ourselves, fail to notice is the quality of our water.
